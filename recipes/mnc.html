Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese is delicious and doesn't take time to assemble. It's always popular, and it is perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • macaroni
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • cheddar cheese
  • evaporated milk
  • eggs
  • whole milk
  • condesed cheddar cheese soup
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with salted water and bring to a boil. Stir in the macaroni and return to a boil. Cook uncovered for 8 minutes. Drain and transfer the pasta to the slow cooker
  2. Add the butter in the pasta and stir until melted. Sprinkle half of the cheddar and stir.
  3. Stir evaporated milk and eggs then stir into pasta.
  4. Whisk milk and the condesed soup together then stir into pasta.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over pasta and garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on low for three hours.
  7. Enjoy!
