Slow Cooker Sausage with Sauce

Description

This recipe is a warm and easy meal, and the slow cooker makes them even easier. It can be served with sandwiches or rice.

Ingredients

  • fresh Italian sausages
  • spaghetti sauce
  • one seeded and sliced green bell pepper
  • one sliced onion
  • six hoagie rolls

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Put the Italian sausages, spaghetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into the slow cooker. Mix untl combined.
  3. Cover and cook on low for six hours. Serve on the hoagie rolls.
