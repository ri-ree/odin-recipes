Fiesta Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Tacos

Description

This recipe makes use of a slow cooker to cook delicious chicken tacos. It is easy, and makes an incredible meal.

Ingredients

  • chicken broth
  • seasoning mix
  • chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Mix the broth and the seasoning mix.
  2. Put the chicken in the slow cooker before before the broth mixture over it.
  3. Cook for 6 to 8 hours on low, then shred the chicken.
